Text Streaming Comparison

About This Demo

This page demonstrates two different approaches to text animation:

  • Direct API Streaming: Text is displayed immediately as it's generated by the AI, creating a more natural and engaging experience.
  • Traditional Animation: The entire response is fetched first, then animated to simulate typing.

The direct streaming approach feels more like a real conversation and keeps users engaged while waiting for responses.
