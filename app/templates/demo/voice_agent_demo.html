About This Demo

PitchIQ's voice demo allows you to practice your sales skills with a dynamically generated persona. Simply start the conversation and begin speaking naturally.

How It Works

  1. Click Start Conversation to begin
  2. Describe your business when prompted
  3. A custom persona will be generated for you
  4. Practice your pitch with the AI prospect
  5. Click End Call when finished

Tips

  • Speak clearly and at a natural pace
  • Allow brief pauses for the AI to respond
  • Use the Mute button if needed during the call
  • Try different approaches with the same persona
  • The conversation transcript is available during the call
